About City General Hospital

Learn about our history, mission, vision, and the team that makes us a leading healthcare provider

Our Story

Founded in 1985, City General Hospital has grown from a small community clinic to a comprehensive medical center serving the healthcare needs of our city and surrounding areas.

Over the years, we have continuously expanded our facilities, enhanced our services, and invested in cutting-edge medical technology to provide the highest quality of care to our patients.

Today, City General Hospital stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence, offering a wide range of specialized medical services delivered by a team of experienced healthcare professionals dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of our community.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, accessible, and quality healthcare services to all members of our community with compassion, dignity, and respect, and to continuously improve the health and wellbeing of the people we serve.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in the region, recognized for clinical excellence, patient-centered care, innovative treatments, and cutting-edge medical technology.

Our Values

Excellence, Compassion, Integrity, Respect, Innovation, Teamwork, and Community Service form the cornerstone of our approach to healthcare delivery.

Our Journey

A look back at our hospital's significant milestones and achievements throughout the years

Hospital Founding

City General Hospital was established as a small community clinic with 20 beds and basic medical services.

1985

Major Expansion

The hospital expanded to 100 beds and added specialized departments including cardiology and pediatrics.

1992

Research Center

Establishment of our medical research center to advance healthcare knowledge and innovative treatments.

2001

Technological Advancement

Implementation of electronic health records and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment.

2010

New Medical Wing

Opening of a new medical wing with 150 additional beds and advanced surgical facilities.

2018

Excellence in Healthcare Award

Recognition for outstanding patient care and medical excellence.

2023
